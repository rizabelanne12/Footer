Our Story

The global outbreak of the COVID-19 pandemic in 2019 reshaped the landscape of how organizations operate, challenging conventional workplace norms. As a response to the evolving needs of businesses, eMachine, an Australian-based organization, is seeking a Hot Desk Booking System to facilitate the gradual return of employees to the office. Our team, composed of third-year students in the BS Information Systems program, is enthusiastic about seizing this opportunity to contribute to the real-world application of our skills and expand our expertise.

While acknowledging that our team may not be seasoned experts in the specific domain, we are committed to leveraging our academic knowledge, innovative thinking, and the desire to learn swiftly. We see this project as an invaluable chance to bridge the gap between theory and practice, demonstrating our adaptability and eagerness to develop the necessary skills for success. Our interdisciplinary backgrounds bring diverse perspectives that can foster creative solutions and agile problem-solving.

In summary, while we may not possess extensive industry experience, our team is eager, adaptable, and committed to delivering a Hot Desk Booking System that aligns with eMachine's vision. We view this opportunity as a platform to enhance our skills, contribute meaningfully, and establish a foundation for future success. We are confident that our passion for learning and dedication to the project will result in a successful partnership between eMachine and our team.

Our Mission

In envisioning the future of workplace adaptability, our project seeks to pioneer a transformative solution for eMachine, encapsulating the essence of a dynamic and flexible work environment. We aspire to develop the Hot Desk Booking System as a catalyst, seamlessly bridging the gap between remote work and the traditional office setting as the solution to encourage the return of the employees to the office.
